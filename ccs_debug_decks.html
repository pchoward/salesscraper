LOADING PRODUCTS

Here at CCS, we are constantly stocking and restocking our warehouse with the newest items from the best brands in skateboarding. But sometimes we order too much stuff and start to run out of space. Sure, we could just stack boxes precariously in the hallways and bathroom, but we thought it would be more fun to offer you some select gear at blow-out prices.

If you are looking for some cheap skate shoes or a discounted skate deck (all in new, perfect condition), the CCS Clearance section is the place to start your search. Take some time to browse around. We think you’ll be pleasantly surprised by what you find. These ain’t no factory seconds or defunct brands. These are the same top-quality pants, shirts, hoodies, hats, hardgoods, and skate shoes on sale. Sometimes as much as 60% off.

Keep in mind, that sometimes sizes are limited and our standard free returns policy does not apply to clearance items. So sit back and take in the deep discounts. It’s a great way to bolster your bag of tricks without breaking your wallet.
